geoBoundaries - A Global Database of Political Administrative Divisions

The geoBoundaries Global Database of Political Administrative Boundaries is the world's largest open license database of boundaries for every country in the world. This database currently consists of approximately 1 million boundaries and includes all 195 UN member states. This publication is also available on Harvard Dataverse.
